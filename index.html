Why this site exists

ReadOnBelieve exists to make one thing simple: opening the Bible and actually reading it, without ads, distractions, or accounts. Every word of every chapter is the real, complete text — nothing is summarized or trimmed.

Your bookmarks and notes stay on your own device. There's no login because there's nothing to keep from you.
